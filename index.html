AN ARUSHA LEGEND

Since 1978, Khan's Barbeque has been the heart of Arusha's culinary scene. Located on the historic Mosque Street, we've perfected the art of Nyama Choma - the authentic Tanzanian barbecue that brings people together.

Our charcoal grill has been serving the same mouth-watering recipes for over four decades, making us not just a restaurant, but a landmark in Arusha's food culture.
